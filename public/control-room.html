Hackathon submission

One engine, three transport modes, live rerouting proof.

GoliTransit is designed for the kind of dense Dhaka navigation standard route planners miss. A realistic trip may begin on a main road by car, continue through a narrow goli by rickshaw, and finish on foot through an overpass. This interface makes that system easy to understand and easy to verify.
